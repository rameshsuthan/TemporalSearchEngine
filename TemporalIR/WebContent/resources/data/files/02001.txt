A second case of Ebola in the United States was diagnosed on October 11, 2014 in a nurse, Nina Pham, who had provided care to Duncan at the hospital. Another nurse who treated Duncan, Amber Joy Vinson, became the third person diagnosed with the disease on October 14, 2014. A fourth case of Ebola was diagnosed on October 23, 2014 in New York City physician Craig Spencer, who had just returned from working with Doctors Without Borders in Guinea, a country in west Africa.
