ON Dec 2, 2014. Ebola is a deadly disease spread through contact with an infected person’s bodily fluids, putting health care workers particularly at risk for infection..four hospitals in Chicago, four in New York City, four throughout California and three in the District of Columbia are among the facilities designated by state health officials as Ebola treatment centers. All of the U.S. hospitals that have successfully treated Ebola patients thus far are on the list.
