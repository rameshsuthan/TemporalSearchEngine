The 40-year-old woman, whose name was not immediately released, died of an apparent heart attack while sitting in a chair at a Brooklyn hair salon. City health officials responded to the scene and cordoned off the building. The woman was one of approximately 350 that the city has been monitoring for Ebola symptoms. The New York City Department of Health confirmed to MyFoxNY.com that the tests on the woman's remains came back negative for the virus.
