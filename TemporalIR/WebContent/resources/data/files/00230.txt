In a statement, G20 leaders meeting in Brisbane, Australia, said member nations were committed to “do what is necessary to ensure the international effort can extinguish the outbreak,” to “promote safe conditions and training for health care and relief workers,” and “expedite the effective and targeted disbursement of funds and other assistance.”Eight of the previous nine Ebola patients treated in the United States survived. Liberian national Thomas Eric Duncan, who was treated in Dallas, died on 15 Oct.
