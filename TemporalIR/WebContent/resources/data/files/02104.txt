Among earlier confirmed U.S. Ebola cases were two Americans who returned already ill from West Africa for treatment at Emory University Hospital in Atlanta. They were treated and released, virus free.In a Gallup  poll of over 1,000 Americans taken on Oct 5, 2014, one fifth were worried about contracting Ebola. Still, the chances you'll be exposed to Ebola in the United States are very low because of the tight infection control practices of public health workers here.
