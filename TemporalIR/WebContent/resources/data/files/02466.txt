On September 30, 2014, the CDC announced that Thomas Eric Duncan, a 42-year-old Liberian national visiting the United States from the west African country of Liberia, had been diagnosed with Ebola in Dallas, Texas. Duncan, who had been visiting family in Dallas, was treated at Texas Health Presbyterian Hospital Dallas.By October 4, Duncan's condition had deteriorated from "serious but stable" to "critical". On October 8, Duncan died of Ebola.
