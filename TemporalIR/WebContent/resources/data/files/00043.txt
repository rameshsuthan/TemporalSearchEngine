Following the confirmation of Ebola Virus Disease (EVD) outbreak in Guinea, the Ministry of Health and Social Welfare (MOHSW) has recorded two episodes of EVD epidemics in less than six months. The first epidemic began on 22 March and ended in April, 2014 and mainly affected two counties. The last case was confirmed on 10 April, 2014. Cumulatively, six cases were confirmed positive of the Ebola virus and all died at the time, (Case Fatality Rate [CFR] of 100%).
